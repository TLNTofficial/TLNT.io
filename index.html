Our pilot community: iCasting

iCasting will be the first platform on which the TLNT will be integrated. Since our launch in 2015, the iCasting platform has exponentially grown to over 140.000 registered talents, making it the biggest casting agency in the Netherlands. Combined with over 1500 clients, we’ve already created a thriving and bustling community of supply and demand.
